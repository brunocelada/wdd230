Who do we work with?

Rosario is a city located in the southeastern part of the province of Santa Fe, Argentina. It serves as the capital of the homonymous department. It is the most populous city in the province and the third-largest in the country, including its metropolitan area.

In 2010, according to data from the 2010 census provided by INDEC (National Institute of Statistics and Censuses), the population of the city proper was 948,312 inhabitants. According to the same census, the population of the Rosario department (comprising the city and 23 additional localities) was 1,193,697 people. This represents 37.36% of the total population of the province of Santa Fe.
