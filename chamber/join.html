Gold Membership

Our Gold Membership represents the pinnacle of engagement and influence within Chamber of Commerce Rosario's construction community. This elite membership level offers unparalleled advantages for businesses aiming to thrive and lead in the industry.

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership is ideal for companies aspiring to enhance their presence and opportunities within the Chamber's construction network. Enjoy a comprehensive package of benefits that will help you grow and excel in the industry.

Bronze Membership

Our Bronze Membership is designed for businesses looking to establish a strong foundation in the local construction industry. This entry-level membership provides essential benefits to kickstart your growth and recognition.

Non profit Membership

The NP (Non-Profit) Membership level is exclusively for non-profit organizations dedicated to the construction sector in Rosario. As a token of our commitment to community development, there are no membership fees associated with this level.
