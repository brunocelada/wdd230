Overview

Site Name

"Rosario Chamber of Commerce"
This name represents a city in which the chamber operates; also, it's the most simple but complete name I thought.

Site Purpose

A Rosario local organization that gathers construction business leaders working in the community.
The intention is to advance the goals and interests operating in Rosario, such as:
-manage and develop fluid institutional relationships with representatives from the public and private sectors.
-generate, promote and support strategic projects that contribute to the economic development of their region of belonging.

Scenarios

▪ What well-known companies in the city have already decided to join the chamber of commerce?
▪ In which areas of the city did the most economic growth occur?
▪ Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors, and how to verify their trustworthiness?
